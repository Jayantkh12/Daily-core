Refund Policy

Effective Date: 02/11/2024

Thank you for shopping at DailyCore! We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied with your order, we are here to help.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

  • Return Window: You may return most items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund. Please note that products must be unused and in their original packaging with all labels and seals intact.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Certain items are non-returnable for health and hygiene reasons. This includes:
    • Opened or used beauty products.
    • Gift cards.
    • Downloadable software products.
  • Damaged or Defective Products: If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will replace the product or provide a full refund.

2. Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Customer Service: Email our customer service team at support@dailycore.com with your order number and the reason for the return.
  2. Return Authorization: Once your request is approved, you will receive a return authorization and instructions on how to return your item.
  3. Pack the Item: Securely package the item in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
  4. Ship the Item: Send the item back to the address provided in the return authorization. Please note that you are responsible for the return shipping costs unless the item is defective or the wrong item was sent.

3. Refund Processing

  • Refund Timeline: Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the refund status. If your return is approved, we will process your refund to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.
  • Shipping Costs: Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you received a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund amount.

4. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges at this time. If you would like a different product, please return the unwanted item for a refund and place a new order.

5. Changes to This Policy

DailyCore reserves the right to amend this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
