Refund Policy

Effective Date: 02/11/2024

Thank you for shopping at DailyCore! If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

  • Return Window: You may return most items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund. Products must be unused and in their original packaging.

  • Damaged or Defective Products: Contact us immediately. We will replace the product or provide a full refund.

2. Return Process

  1. Email jayantchoudhary2901@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return.

3. Refund Processing

  • Refund Timeline: Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days of receiving the returned item.

We do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the unwanted item for a refund and place a new order.
