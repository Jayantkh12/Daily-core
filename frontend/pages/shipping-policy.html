Shipping Policy

We strive to provide the best shipping experience to ensure your products reach you in perfect condition and on time.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

2. Shipping Rates and Delivery Times

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day (select areas)

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to weather, carrier delays, or high demand.

3. International Shipping

DailyCore ships to select countries. Customs fees, duties, and taxes for international shipments are the customer's responsibility.

4. Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to P.O. Boxes or certain remote locations. Contact jayantchoudhary2901@gmail.com for concerns.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email. Please allow 24 hours for tracking updates.
