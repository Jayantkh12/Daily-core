Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping at DailyCore. We strive to provide the best shipping experience to ensure your products reach you in perfect condition and on time. Please review our Shipping Policy to understand how we handle deliveries.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

2. Shipping Rates and Delivery Times

Shipping costs and delivery estimates are calculated at checkout based on your location and chosen shipping method. Estimated delivery times are as follows:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day delivery (available in select areas)

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to factors such as weather, carrier delays, or high demand.

4. Shipping Restrictions

DailyCore currently does not ship to P.O. Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or certain remote locations. For any shipping concerns, please contact our support team at jayantchoudhary2901@gmail.com.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email. Please allow 24 hours for tracking updates to be reflected on the carrier’s website.

6. Delays and Issues

If there is a significant delay in your order's shipment, we will contact you by email. While we aim for timely delivery, DailyCore is not responsible for shipping delays caused by weather, carrier issues, or other circumstances beyond our control.

7. Missing or Lost Packages

If your tracking information indicates that your package has been delivered but you haven’t received it, please check with neighbors or other locations. If your package is still missing, contact us within 7 days of the delivery date, and we’ll assist with filing a claim with the carrier.
